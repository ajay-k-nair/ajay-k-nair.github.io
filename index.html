About Me

I am a PhD Student at Department of Mathematics, Indian Institute of Science Bangalore. My advisor is Prof. Subhojoy Gupta with Prof. Siddhartha Gadgil as a co-advisor. I am working broadly in the field of low-dimensional geometry/topology specialising in geometric structures on surfaces and their holonomies. Please see here for detailed information about my research interests and here for my publications.

I am presently a co-organiser of Students' Topology Geometry Seminars (STGS). I was past organiser of Prime Time Seminars at IISc.
