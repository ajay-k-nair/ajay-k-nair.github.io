About Me

I am a PhD Student at Department of Mathematics, Indian Institute of Science Bangalore. My advisor is Prof. Subhojoy Gupta with Prof. Siddhartha Gadgil as a co-advisor. I am working broadly in the field of low-dimensional geometry/topology specialising in geometric structures on surfaces and their holonomies.

More recently, I have developed a strong interest in the formalisation of mathematics.
